USAspending.gov is operated by the Treasury Department and receives data
from a variety of government source systems outside of Treasury. USAspending.gov
does not separately validate the data collected on external source systems;
USAspending.gov relies on the data validation and internal controls of
these external source systems. More information about Broker validation
rules and SAO certification can be found in the "DAIMS Validation Rules"
and "DAIMS Practices and Procedures" links on the
<a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="https://fiscal.treasury.gov/data-transparency/resources.html">resources page for the DATA Act Information Model Schema (DAIMS)</a>.
